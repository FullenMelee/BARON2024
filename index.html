Roasted Maple Neck

A feature of most RSM series is the Roasted Maple Neck. By heating the maple, the stability of the neck is improved. This gives the neck more stability and produces a more resonant tone.

Global Series

Spec points

Roasted Maple Neck



A feature of most RSM series is the Roasted Maple Neck. By heating the maple, the stability of the neck is improved. This gives the neck more stability and produces a more resonant tone.


All models

33-inch models

We bring 33" scale models, which have slightly shorter lengths than most basses. The tension is slightly looser, this makes it easier for players with smaller hands to hold down the strings and challenge their technical playing style. This will allow you to realize how fun it is to play!

Electric Basses

Locking tuner and Wilkinson 2 point Tremolo

Electric guitar models feature a locking tuner for tuning stability and a Wikinson 2 point tremolo for dynamic arming.

Electric Guitars

25-inch models

We bring 25" scale models, which is between "Long Scale" and "Medium Scale". The tension is a little looser than a typical bolt-on guitar, making it easier for players with small hands and beginners to hold down the strings. It also makes it easier to play with choking and vibrato, making playing the guitar more fun.

Electric Guitars

Quality control in Japan




All products of the Universe series and Global series are quality controlled by our QC team in Japan before being shipped.


All models
